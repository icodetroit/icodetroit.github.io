Get Inspired

Inspiration is all around and often times it simply takes an open heart and open mind to take it in.

Within the pages of this site, Imaginarium Creative Outreach hopes to inspire others to uplift and empower themselves and their communities.

Using Detroit as a vessel, ICO will explore and find new ways of keeping this creative community alive and inspired.

Get Involved

Imaginarium Creative Outreach will do its best to raise awareness for all the opportunities Detroiters have to inspire and uplift their communities through the arts. Not only will ICO hosts projects and initiatives, we will also shine light on the incredible acts happening with local organizations, programs, and people.
